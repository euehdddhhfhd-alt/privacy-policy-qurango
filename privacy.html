Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2024-05-22

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your data. We believe in transparency and your right to privacy.

Data Collection and Usage

The app is a fully offline application.

  • Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Usage Data: We do not track your usage of the app, which pages you visit, or how you interact with the content.
  • Device Information: We do not collect identifiers or technical details about your device.

Ads and Analytics

This app does not contain any advertisements and does not use any analytics services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase). Your activity remains entirely private on your device.

Third-Party Services

Since the app operates offline, we do not share any data with third-party service providers. No data is transmitted from your device to any external servers.

Permissions

The app may request minimal permissions required for its core functionality. These permissions are used solely to provide the services within the app and never to collect or transmit your data.

Changes to this policy

If we make any changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top. We encourage you to review this page occasionally.
